About KP

KP Connect is a workforce development, education, and community-impact platform built on a simple idea: when skills are valued, shared, and rewarded, individuals and communities grow together. We connect individuals, employers, educators, funders, nonprofits, and government agencies inside one supportive ecosystem.

From scholars and apprentices to employers and funders, every participant has a place in the talent supply chain — and a path to opportunity.
