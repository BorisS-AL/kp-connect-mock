Talent Supply Chains

The community stakeholder model allows organizations to collaborate in a very structured manner on the common objective of producing talent for employers. This demand-driven system is customized to deliver talent with the skills required for the positions available today and into the future. Based upon the principles of supply-chain theory, this approach is specifically designed to address talent supply–demand misalignment.
