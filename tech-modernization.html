Overview

The Technology Modernization Grant (TMG) is designed to address the critical IT and cybersecurity talent gap facing organizations across the United States. With a commitment of $750,000 for 2025, the program provides participating organizations with up to $15,000 in valuable services, coupled with a six-month membership to the Kinetic Potential Network (KPN) — equipping organizations with the resources and talent needed to thrive in an increasingly digital landscape.
