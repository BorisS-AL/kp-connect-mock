What is KP Network?

The Kinetic Potential (KP) Network is a membership-based community that increases your organization's capacity to execute its mission, achieve sustainable growth, and meet the demands of an expanding workforce. As the integrator, KP provides the operational platform and talent supply chain to connect you with educators, employers, nonprofits, funders, and government agencies — all collaborating to ensure you have the talent and resources needed to succeed.
